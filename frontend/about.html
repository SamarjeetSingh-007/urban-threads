Our Story

Where street culture meets premium fashion

Born in Mumbai, Built for India

Urban Threads started in Bandra, Mumbai, where street culture, music, and local creativity naturally blend. From day one, our goal has been simple: create premium streetwear that feels global in quality but rooted in Indian identity.

Our design language is inspired by everyday India — monsoon textures, metro movement, campus fashion, and late-night city energy. We obsess over fit, fabric, and finish so every piece works in real Indian weather and real Indian wardrobes.

Today, Urban Threads serves a growing community across Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad, and beyond. We are more than a clothing label — we are a homegrown streetwear movement made for the next generation of Indian creators.
