Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (such as account details and order data), plus limited technical data required to operate and secure the website.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to process orders, provide support, improve user experience, and communicate essential service updates.

3. Payments

Payment transactions are processed through secure third-party providers. Urban Threads does not store full card details on its servers.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies for session continuity, cart persistence, and performance insights.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with trusted service providers for logistics, payments, and platform operations.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, misuse, and disclosure.

7. Your Rights

You may request updates or deletion of your account information, subject to legal and operational retention requirements.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version is always available on this page.

9. Contact

For privacy requests, please contact us via our support page.
